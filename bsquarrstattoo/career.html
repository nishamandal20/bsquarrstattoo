Manager

We’re looking for a manager for our studio, which manages the day-to-day operations between our artists and clients.
We’re looking for someone who believes that each and every customer deserves
world-class service and goes above and beyond to make sure that service is delivered.
Studio managers have a demanding job, but the demands are worth the rewards.
This is one of those rare opportunities where your work will help enrich others lives.

Your day-to-day :

  • Manage customer queries adhering to a daily schedule that includes a mix of: Phones,
    three simultaneous chats, and emails ensuring clients have a quick response time with same-day replies
  • Delight our customers. You’ll pull out all the stops to deliver a memorable experience
    (in a “I can’t wait to tell my friends about this fabulous service” way)
  • Research social media platforms and generate reports for the same.
  • Additional store manager duties as needed

We’d love to hear from you if you have :

  • Proven track record in managing teams; distributed teams a plus
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills in English and Hindi.
  • Not only will you talk to people on the phone,
    you’ll communicate with our customers in writing as well.
  • Must have a friendly and welcoming nature.
  • Self-starter, who can work independently and enthusiastically to ensure customer success
  • Highly organised and detail-oriented
  • Applicants who are enthusiastic about the Tattoo business is a plus.

Head of Marketing

We are looking for a Head of Marketing who’ll lead all our marketing activities from
social media and digital campaigns to advertising and creative projects.

Head of Marketing responsibilities include developing plans to help establish our brand,
allocating resources to different projects and setting short-term and long-term department goals.
If you’re a skilled Marketing strategist, able to inspire your team members, we’d like to meet you.

Ultimately, you will run our Marketing department in ways that promote higher profitability and competitiveness.

Your day-to-day :

  • Craft strategies for all Marketing teams, including Digital, Advertising, Communications and Creative
  • Prepare and manage monthly, quarterly and annual budgets for the Marketing department
  • Set, monitor and report on team goals
  • Design branding, positioning and pricing strategies
  • Ensure our brand message is strong and consistent across all channels and marketing efforts
    (like events, email campaigns, web pages and promotional material)
  • Analyze consumer behavior and determine customer personas
  • Identify opportunities to reach new market segments and expand market share
  • Monitor competition (acquisitions, pricing changes and new tattoo trends and features)
  • Coordinate sales and marketing efforts to boost brand awareness

We’d love to hear from you if you have :

  • Work experience as Head of Marketing
  • Experience running successful marketing campaigns
  • Solid knowledge of web analytics, Facebook and Instagram ads, Google Adwords
  • Experience with CRM software
  • Leadership skills with the ability to set and prioritise goals
  • Analytical mind

Graphic Designer

We are looking for a Graphic Designer to join our creative team to help bring our brand to life and evolve our design vision.
This role will contribute to a wide range of projects with work spanning both the digital and physical
space — anything from email and site design to in-store signage and paid advertising is fair game.
This position will work closely with the marketing team to develop, concept and execute brand campaigns.

Your day-to-day :

  • Produce innovative and beautiful design solutions consistent with our brand image across site,
    social media, paid and physical channels
  • Collaborate with creative and marketing team members to create campaign-level work across multiple
    social media channels and customer touchpoints (Facebook & Instagram ad visuals)
  • Set, monitor and report on team goals
  • Design branding, positioning and pricing strategies

We’d love to hear from you if you have :

  • 3+ years experience within the field of design
  • A strong portfolio showcasing work across a range of platforms: digital, print, paid, packaging and more
  • Expert knowledge of typography, grid systems, colour theory and composition.
    A background in motion graphics and illustration is a bonus
  • A passion for brand building and a strong understanding of how to maintain and evolve design systems
  • Advanced Creative Suite and Sketch knowledge. AfterEffects proficiency is a bonus
  • Previous experience at an agency or high end consumer brand preferred, but not required
  • Proven leadership and strong communication skills plus an ability to work cross functionally with ease

Email Marketing Manager

We are looking for a talented Email marketing manager to take the lead with our email marketing strategies.
Your main duties include running email marketing campaigns end-to-end, managing email databases and creating newsletters.

If you’re interested in web technologies and can generate innovative ideas to increase sales, we would like to meet you.
As an Email marketing manager, your role is to reach customers through email campaigns
and inform them about new tattoos, trends and events.
Ultimately, you should be able to promote our brand and ensure our customers stay in touch with our company updates.

Your day-to-day :

  • Identify target audience and grow our email list
  • Design and implement direct email marketing campaigns
  • Ensure mobile-friendly email templates
  • Write newsletters including all company updates
  • Upgrade our email templates using graphics, personalisation and advanced features
  • Ensure prompt and accurate communication with clients via email to minimize unsubscribes
  • Create email databases for lead generation
  • Analyse campaign performance and suggest improvements
  • Report on sales revenue generated from email marketing efforts
  • Ensure emails follow industry policies and best practices

We’d love to hear from you if you have :

  • Proven work experience as an Email marketing manager or Digital marketing specialist
  • Hands on experience with HTML, CSS and content management systems
  • Proficiency in marketing automation technology
  • Knowledge of SEO/SEM and Google Analytics
  • Familiarity with analytical and database tools
  • Excellent written communication and copywriting skills
  • Strong project management skills
  • An ability to work under tight deadlines
